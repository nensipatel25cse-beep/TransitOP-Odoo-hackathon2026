HOW IT WORKS

Step-by-Step Workflow

See how our system enforces mandatory business rules at every stage of a trip.

1

Create Trip Request

Dispatcher enters cargo details, selects vehicle and driver. System automatically validates:

  • ✓ Vehicle registration is unique
  • ✓ Vehicle is Available (not In Shop/Retired)
  • ✓ Driver license is valid and not suspended
2

Validate Load

System checks cargo weight against vehicle capacity (Rule 5).

Example: Cargo = 800kg | Vehicle Capacity = 1200kg → Approved

3

Dispatch Trip

On successful validation, trip is dispatched and statuses updated automatically (Rule 6).

Vehicle & Driver status changed to On Trip
4

Trip Execution & Completion

Upon delivery, trip is marked complete → statuses restored to Available (Rule 7).
